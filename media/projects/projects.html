Title Cake or Die LDoT Vortex Math Tutor
Image
Description As part of my senior project at Weber State myself and a two other students put together an FPS that is more chalenging than it lets on This started out a Unity Tutorial which evolved over several months to include 10 levels, 2 bosses, 13 different enemies and 7 power ups. I scripted all the animations and visual effects. The back end is all origianal work with multiple lives, scoring, and invulnrability. This is an all original database aplication designed to help my wife with her clients and the stones she uses in focusing energy of gems and crystals. I was commissioned to write a simple math tutor aplication for use at a preschool and day care. The achievement of this aplication is the security hashes that I wrote myself. While simple and not industry standard they work very well.
